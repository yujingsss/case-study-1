Memory Möbius "Waves"

Möbius

Distorted identity seeker

Focus on the theme of identity seeking. Using a variety of materials and visual signifiers, this sculpture is a representation showing how the cyborgs struggling to find their true selves. The research of this project is based on Blade Runner and Ghost In The Shell.

  • Möbius
  • Individual Work
  • Sculpture
  • Foam, Form Wire, Air Dry Clay, Liquid Plastic
  • 2019

    • Artificial intelligence is the invention of humankind that reflect the human desire of endless identity seeking.

    The most common idea expressed in fiction is the conflict of identity. Both internal(self) & external(human vs cyborg) conflicts are presented in the future world.
